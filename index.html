About

I'm an AI/ML Engineer with a deep interest in making machine learning systems work reliably in the real world. My work spans model training, infrastructure, and the tooling that lets teams move fast without breaking things.

Previously I've worked on large language models, recommendation systems, and ML platform engineering — always with an eye toward scalability, observability, and clean abstractions. I care about code quality as much as model quality.

When I'm not at a computer, I'm probably reading, hiking, or arguing about the right way to structure a data pipeline.
